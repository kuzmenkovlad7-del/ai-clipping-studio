AI Clipping Studio

Upload one long video → get 9:16 clips with pro subtitles, delivered to your Telegram.

From long talk to viral shorts — fully automated.

  • We detect the strongest emotional moments and conflicts in your speech.
  • Each clip is cut to vertical 9:16 with blurred background and burned-in subtitles.
  • You choose language and clip length — we handle the rest.

How it works

  1. Open Telegram and start @AIClippingStudioBot.
  2. Come back here and enter your Telegram @username.
  3. Upload a long video file and click Start AI Clipping.
  4. When clips are ready, the bot sends all files directly to your Telegram chat.
We only send compressed audio to OpenAI, so even multi-GB videos are supported — you just need enough disk space on the server.
Important: Before you submit, open Telegram, find @AIClippingStudioBot and press Start. Otherwise the bot will not be allowed to send you the clips.
